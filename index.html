Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Classroom & Real Research

At Project Q2, we are working to empower students to become critical thinkers, problem-solvers, and effective communicators by providing academic resources, guidance, and mentorship opportunities to youth of all ages.

Unfortunately, the reality for many high school students is that research programs and opportunities are limited or often inaccessible. Current school programs tend to focus more on standardized testing and basic coursework, leaving little room for the development of independent research skills. As a result, students often lack the resources, guidance, and mentorship necessary to engage in meaningful research. This can stifle creativity, reduce opportunities for personal growth, and ultimately hinder students from reaching their full potential.

Project Q2 seeks to change that by providing students with the tools and opportunities to explore their passions and push the boundaries of their education. Ultimately, we aim to bridge the gap between the classroom and real-world applications and foster curiosity and academic growth.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions? Reach out and we'll respond within 48 hours.

What is the price for REPS mentorship?
+
As an organization focused on fostering egalitarianism in education, we believe that all youth should have access to our services. With this, mentorship with REPS is completely FREE of cost. We ask that you reference our assistance on any official publications that involved mentorship/services from Project Q2.
How can I donate to The Q2 Foundation?
+
Please use our 'Contact Us' form and we will get back to you in regards to how you may donate. If your donation is not monetary funding, please directly contact Blakedjbs@gmail.com.
What qualifications do REPS mentors have?
+
Our REPS mentors come from various academic backgrounds, from publishing in a Q2 journal to interning and conducting research at top-notch university facilities. Additionally, our mentors have undergone specific education training. We can assure you that you will be paired with the best-of-the-best youth researchers.
How do I get involved with Project Q2 as a contributor?
+
We are currently accepting youth volunteers for various positions, including blog management, SEO development, and funding coordination. Please use the 'Contact Us' form to apply to join Project Q2. We are looking forward to having you in our team!
Are need-based scholarships available for courses?
+
Yes. Need-based scholarships are available, ensuring access for individuals from all backgrounds. Contact us via the form to discuss eligibility and the application process.
